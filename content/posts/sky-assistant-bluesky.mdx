## What is Sky Assistant?
[Sky Assistant](https://www.skyassistant.app) is a powerful automation tool designed to help you build and engage with your audience on BlueSky. 
It intelligently handles interactions responding to content to maintain an active presence while you focus on creating great content.
With smart filtering and scheduling capabilities, Sky Assistant ensures your engagement feels natural and meaningful.

## Features and Benefits
Sky Assistant makes it easy to grow your BlueSky presence with features like automated engagement, smart content discovery, and detailed analytics to track your growth. 
Whether you're an individual creator or managing a brand account, Sky Assistant helps you save time while building genuine connections in the BlueSky community.
