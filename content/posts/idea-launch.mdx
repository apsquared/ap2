## ⚙️ How It Works

The flow is simple on purpose:

1. **Share your idea.** Tell us what you're building, who it's for, and what action you want visitors to take (sign up, join a waitlist, click a button).
2. **We build and launch the test.** We design the landing page, write the ad creative, run it on Meta against your target audience, and track every click and conversion.
3. **You get a clean readout.** In a few days, you get the metrics that actually matter — CTR, conversion rate, cost per signup — plus a plain-English recommendation on whether the signal is strong enough to keep building.

No subscription. No ads-manager learning curve. **One flat fee of $49 per ad run.**

The point of Idea Launch isn't a pretty dashboard — it's *signal*. Real people, with real wallets, deciding in real time whether your pitch is interesting enough to click.
